## Personal Loan Policy

- Complete digital and paperless process from login to disbursement; case can be disbursed within 48 hrs subject to all documents correctly submitted
- Real time tracking of application status from login to disbursement on DSA portal
- Minimum net income required:

| City | Min net monthly salary |
|------|------------------------|
| Mumbai | 35k |
| Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Ahmedabad | 25k |
| Other cities | 25k |

- Minimum CIBIL score 700 subject to customer profile
- Loan amount between 1 Lac to 30 Lacs
- Tenure - Max tenure upto 72 months
- FOIR upto 80% for Super Cat A, Cat A, Cat B, Cat B & Govt.
- Unsecured enquiries - max 5 in last 3m and max 3 in last 1m
- Cibil -1 (NTC segment):
  - Max loan amount of 10 Lacs for Super Cat A & Cat A
  - Max loan amount of 5 Lacs for other company categories
- No sourcing from Partnership or proprietorship firm employees allowed
- Government Group C allowed for certain designations; Group D not allowed
- Min salary for govt teachers >50K with a min experience of 2 yrs (5 yrs for Kolkata)
- **Balance Transfer:** Personal Loan BT and OD BT with min 6 EMIs paid and min cashout of 1 Lac
- **Variable income components:**
  - Incentive
  - Bonus
  - Rental income can be clubbed if there is a valid and registered rent agreement
  - Pension Income can be considered at 100% if recurring with a valid proof if customer is within max age bracket
